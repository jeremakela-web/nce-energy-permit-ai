# Poland Environmental Impact Assessment (OOŚ) — GDOS

## Regulatory Authority

GDOŚ (Generalna Dyrekcja Ochrony Środowiska / General Directorate for Environmental Protection) is Poland's central environmental protection authority, located at Al. Jerozolimskie 136, 02-305 Warszawa.

## Environmental Conditioning Decision (Decyzja o Środowiskowych Uwarunkowaniach, DŚU)

All energy projects in Poland requiring EIA must obtain a DŚU (environmental conditioning decision) before applying for building permits or concessions. This is a prerequisite for:
- Wind farms
- Solar power plants (above thresholds)
- BESS installations (if meeting area/power thresholds)
- Hydropower plants
- Nuclear facilities (SMR)
- Data centres (if large scale)

## Environmental Impact Assessment (OOŚ) Process

GDOŚ manages environmental impact assessments (Oceny Oddziaływania na Środowisko, OOŚ):

1. Screening (kwalifikowanie): Determine if full EIA is required
2. Scoping (ustalenie zakresu): Define scope of assessment
3. Prepare EIA Report (Raport OOŚ)
4. Public consultation
5. RDOŚ (Regional Director for Environmental Protection) or GDOŚ issues DŚU

## Transboundary Procedures

GDOŚ handles transboundary EIA procedures (procedury transgraniczne OOŚ) for projects that may have cross-border environmental impacts.

## Natura 2000

GDOŚ oversees Poland's Natura 2000 network. Energy projects in or near Natura 2000 areas require additional Habitats Directive assessments (ocena habitatowa).

## Nature Conservation Register

GDOŚ maintains the Central Register of Nature Protection Forms (Centralny Rejestr Form Ochrony Przyrody), which must be consulted before siting energy projects.

## Applicable Legislation

- Ustawa z dnia 3 października 2008 r. o udostępnianiu informacji o środowisku i jego ochronie, udziale społeczeństwa w ochronie środowiska oraz o ocenach oddziaływania na środowisko (EIA Act 2008)
- Dyrektywa OOŚ (EU EIA Directive) — implemented in Polish law
- Prawo ochrony środowiska (Environmental Protection Law)
