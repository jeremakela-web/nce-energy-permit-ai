# Data Centre Energy Performance Reporting — Sweden

## Legal Framework

Sweden's Law 2025:570 mandates annual energy performance disclosure for data centre operators. The framework implements the revised Energy Efficiency Directive (EED, EU).

## Reporting Threshold

Mandatory for facilities with "an installed IT power requirement of at least 500 kW."

Exceptions: Security-sensitive operations, defense, and crisis management facilities.

## Critical Deadlines

- April 27, 2026: Operators must notify Energimyndigheten (Swedish Energy Agency)
- May 15, 2026: Data submission to the EU database must be completed
- Reporting period: Calendar year 2025

## Required Data Parameters

Operators report metrics including:
- Energy consumption
- Water usage
- Waste heat recovery
- Grid services contributions

Parameters specified by Delegated Regulation 2024/1364, Annexes I and II.

## Registration Process

1. Email company details to Energimyndigheten
2. Create EU Login account
3. Await registration confirmation
4. Request data submitter access in EU database
5. Await authorization verification
6. Begin reporting in EU platform

## Policy Framework

- Article 12 of revised EED: Transparency requirements
- Article 12.4: Operators managing facilities >1 MW encouraged to adopt EU Code of Conduct for Data Centre Energy Efficiency

## Voluntary Energy Efficiency Obligations

Under the Energy Efficiency Directive, Sweden requires companies with annual energy consumption over 10 TJ to conduct energy audits or implement certified energy management systems.

## Building Permits

Data centres require building permits (bygglov) under Plan- och bygglagen (PBL). Large installations (typically >5,000 m² or significant impact) require detailed plan (detaljplan) from municipality. Environmental assessment (MKB) under Miljöbalken may be required for particularly large facilities.

## Key Legislation

- Lag 2025:570 om datacenterrapportering (reporting law)
- EU Energy Efficiency Directive (revised 2023/1791/EU)
- Plan- och bygglagen (PBL) — building permits
- Miljöbalken — environmental assessment
