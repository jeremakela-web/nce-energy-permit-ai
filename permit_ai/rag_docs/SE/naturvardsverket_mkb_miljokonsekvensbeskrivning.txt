# Environmental Impact Assessment (MKB) in Sweden — Naturvårdsverket

## Overview

Under Miljöbalken (Environmental Code) Chapter 6, most energy production projects require a Miljökonsekvensbeskrivning (MKB — Environmental Impact Assessment report) before permits can be granted.

## Scope — Which Projects Require MKB

Projects with potentially significant environmental impact require MKB. Typically:
- Wind power plants (vindkraftverk) — all large-scale installations
- Solar power plants (solkraftverk) — above certain size thresholds
- Battery energy storage (batterilager) — larger installations near sensitive areas
- Hydropower installations (vattenkraftverk)
- Nuclear facilities (kärntekniska anläggningar) — mandatory full EIA

## MKB Contents (Miljöbalken Chapter 6, § 35)

The MKB must describe:
1. Alternative sites and project alternatives
2. Current environmental conditions (nollalternativ)
3. Significant impacts on humans, fauna, flora, soil, water, air, climate, landscape, material assets, and cultural heritage
4. Interactions between factors
5. Short-term, long-term, temporary and permanent impacts
6. Mitigation measures (skyddsåtgärder)
7. Impact monitoring plan (kontrollprogram)
8. Non-technical summary

## Process

1. Samråd (consultation): Developer notifies affected parties and authorities; public and stakeholder input collected
2. Undersökning (screening): Authority decides if significant environmental impact likely
3. Prepare MKB
4. Submit application with MKB to permitting authority (Länsstyrelse or Mark- och miljödomstol)
5. Review and decision

## For Energy Projects

Wind power: MKB submitted as part of the application to Mark- och miljödomstol (for concession under Miljöbalken or Ellagen)
Solar power: MKB may be required for installations above Natura 2000 or protected areas thresholds
BESS: MKB required if sited in sensitive areas or combined with other significant installations
Nuclear: Full EIA under Kärntekniklagen with SSM oversight

## Key Legislation

- Miljöbalken (Environmental Code), Chapter 6
- Förordning (1998:905) om miljökonsekvensbeskrivningar
- EU EIA Directive (2011/92/EU as amended by 2014/52/EU)
